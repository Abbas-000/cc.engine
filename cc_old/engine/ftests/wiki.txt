Wiki License
------------

This is a sanity check of the Wiki license interface and deeds.  The wiki
license simply redirects to the results page and presents a slightly 
customized Attribution-ShareAlike license.

  >>> browser = Browser()
  >>> browser.raiseHttpErrors = True
  >>> browser.open('http://localhost:8080/license/wiki')

  >>> browser.url
  'http://localhost:8080/license/results-one?license_code=by-sa&wiki=true'

  The wiki license has a special badge available:

  >>> 'http://creativecommons.org/images/public/cc-wiki.png' in \
  ... browser.contents
  True
