Developing Nations License
--------------------------

The Developing Nations (2.0) license was deprecated in early 2007.

  >>> browser = Browser()
  >>> browser.raiseHttpErrors = True
  >>> browser.open('http://localhost:8080/licenses/devnations/2.0/')
  
  >>> "Developing Nations" in browser.contents
  True
  >>> """This license is <a href="/retiredlicenses">retired</a>""" \
  ... in browser.contents
  True
